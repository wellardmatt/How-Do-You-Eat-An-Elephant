About

I am a graduate in Mathematics from The University of Nottingham, 2005. I have been tutoring over the last 8 years and spent a year working as a Mathematics Tutor at The University of Reading (2012-13).

This year I ran a week long Maths course for Kings School, Oxford for 2 groups of 14 gifted Italian students that were visiting in their summer vacation.

I've had over 50 private tutees over the past 8 years, ranging from short term exam-prep sessions to long term sessions that offer additional support alongside studies at school or college. In longer sessions the student and I build up a comprehensive understanding of the work they are doing on their course, and fill any holes in knowledge we find along the way.

I believe in guided teaching. I don't provide direct answers but rather ask questions so that the student starts to practice their own framework for thinking through a problem themselves. My teaching method involves a mix of discussion on a topic; explanation and reasoning, followed by examples and reference back to our initial discussion. The best way to be able to manipulate a concept and solve mathematical problems is to have a full understanding of the processes you are using and why they work.
