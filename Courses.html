EASTER REVISION COURSES

16th March - 15th April

Easter revision courses will be broken down into module groups each day, covering material from a specific module or area. There will be 2 sessions a day each being 3 hours long.

  • The first of the two will cover material and any problems the students have highlighted, going through questions and theory.

  • The second section will focus on problem solving annd exam questions.

There will be a maximum of 12 students per session and you can book anything from one session to 6 days (covering all areas)
